# 🧙 D&D Character Sheet Web App

A **web application** designed to make creating your **Dungeons & Dragons character sheet** simple and fun.  
No more struggling with complex math or endless tables — just choose your character’s basics, assign stats, and let the app handle the calculations for you.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📝 **Easy Character Creation**: Fill in the essential aspects of your character (name, race, class, background).
- 🔢 **Automated Calculations**: The app does the math for modifiers, saving throws, skill bonuses, and more.
- 📜 **Character Sheet**: Generates a semi-complete sheet with the core details.
- ⚡ **Fast & Intuitive**: Focus on roleplaying instead of crunching numbers.
